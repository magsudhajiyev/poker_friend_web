Features

Gesture-Based Hand Logging

Log your hands during the session with easy and intuitive gesture based input. Just master the gestures and Poker Friend will take care of the rest! Poker Friend will adjust the button position when seat is open, he will also calculate side pots, detect all in hands. You don't have to worry about anything. Just keep logging!

  • Single tap for bet
  • Double tap for check
  • Line for fold
  • L for raise
  • O (circle) for call

Track Opponents

Add your opponent in the app so you can track their game play, understand data driven results after your sessions with them and develop custom exploit strategies to gain edge for each given type of player!

Detailed Statistics

View comprehensive statistics and trends over time with charts, graphs, and detailed breakdowns of your poker performance.

Hand Details

Get comprehensive hand analysis with detailed breakdowns of every decision. Review betting patterns, pot odds, and strategic insights to improve your game and learn from every session.
